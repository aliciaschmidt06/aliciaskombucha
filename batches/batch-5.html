Batch #5

Brewed: 2025-06-11 100% Black tea base

Batch Notes: Very carbonated after 2 days.

  • Strawberry Rhubarb  

    Very fizzy — like, exploding. Good balance; surprisingly not super sweet (tart rhubarb) even though lots of sugar. Herby.

  • Strawberry Mint  

    Smooth strawberry, kick of mint, good balance.

  • Plum  

    Tart, slight vinegar, minimal plum flavour (tastes like plain tart kombucha).

  • Hops + Lime  

    Light, watery beer.

  • Cherry  

    Hints of vinegar, rich bordering wine flavour, retains sweet cherry taste.

  • Apple Cinnamon  

    Explosive, smooth, subtle, not too sweet; smells like apple pie filling (¾ Fuji apple + half stick cinnamon).
